Who We Are

Eden Economy is an engineering-first initiative focused on giving indie companies practical access to advanced manufacturing capability through open systems and structured technical standards.

We design production-ready machine architecture, open component libraries, and software workflows that allow small teams to operate with the discipline and output of larger organizations.

Our Operating Model

Mission

We reduce the cost barrier to serious manufacturing by combining open-source machine architecture with structured documentation and operational software.

How We Work

Our team develops modular machine subsystems, validated part libraries, and integration methods that let founders assemble equipment around real production targets.

Who We Serve

We build for builders: indie hardware teams, startup labs, and engineering-focused businesses that need reliable tools without enterprise-level upfront spending.

With our open libraries and software stack, teams can specify, build, and run the exact machines they need for fabrication, testing, and scale-up. The goal is simple: make high-end engineering infrastructure available to independent companies that are ready to ship real products.

Circuit Board Development

We build machines that can build any curcuit board in the world, including modular expansion cards to modern mother boards.

CAM Software & DFM Workstation — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

High-performance workstation running industry-standard CAM software for Gerber processing, panelization, design-rule checking, and net verification. Every production board passes through this station first to catch errors before any material is committed to the fabrication line.

Laser Direct Imaging System — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Writes circuit patterns directly onto photoresist-coated copper panels using a focused UV laser beam, eliminating the need for film masks entirely. Achieves trace and space widths below 50 microns with perfect registration, enabling high-density interconnect board production for modern computing hardware.

Multilayer Lamination Press — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A hydraulic vacuum press that bonds multiple copper foil and prepreg layers under precisely controlled heat and pressure to produce multilayer PCB stackups. Handles boards up to 20+ layers with controlled impedance, which is standard for motherboards, GPU cards, and high-speed computing interfaces.

Automated Conveyorized Etching Line — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A fully enclosed, high-throughput chemical etching system with automated panel loading, calibrated spray nozzles, multi-stage rinse chambers, and chemical regeneration. Delivers consistent copper removal across entire production panels with tight etch-factor control for fine-pitch computing board traces.

Electroless Copper Deposition Line — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Deposits a thin seed layer of copper inside drilled via holes and onto panel surfaces through chemical reduction. This establishes the base conductivity required for subsequent electrolytic plating and is the critical step that makes multilayer board interlayer connections possible.

Electrolytic Copper Plating Line — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Builds up copper thickness on traces, pads, and inside plated through-holes using electrodeposition. Ensures uniform copper distribution that meets IPC Class 2 and Class 3 specifications for production computing boards, with precise control over plating thickness and throwing power into high-aspect-ratio vias.

High-Speed CNC Drilling Machine — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A multi-spindle mechanical drilling system that creates through-holes, component mounting holes, and mechanical vias at production speed. Achieves positional accuracy within 25 microns with spindle speeds up to 150,000 RPM, handling the thousands of drill hits required per panel on dense computing boards.

Laser Via Drilling System — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A UV or CO2 laser system for creating microvias, blind vias, and buried vias in HDI board designs. Essential for modern computing hardware where mechanical drills cannot achieve the sub-100-micron diameters required for BGA fan-out routing on processors, memory modules, and high-speed interconnects.

Automated Solder Mask Coating Line — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A curtain or spray coating system that applies liquid photoimageable solder mask across entire production panels, followed by UV exposure through a mask, alkaline development, and thermal cure. Produces the professional green, black, or custom-color finish expected on commercial computing products.

Surface Finish Line (ENIG / HASL) — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Applies the final surface finish to exposed copper pads before assembly. Electroless Nickel Immersion Gold for fine-pitch BGA and QFN pads on computing boards, or Hot Air Solder Leveling for standard through-hole and larger SMT applications. Ensures solderability and long shelf life.

CNC Router & V-Score Depanelizer — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Separates individual boards from production panels using precision CNC routing or v-score breaking with controlled depth cuts. Includes integrated dust extraction and board handling conveyors to produce clean, burr-free edges on finished boards ready for assembly.

Bare Board AOI & Electrical Test — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Automated optical inspection and flying probe electrical testing of fabricated bare boards before they enter the assembly line. Verifies trace integrity, copper isolation, via connectivity, and impedance compliance to catch fabrication defects before components are placed.

Automated Solder Paste Printer — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A fully automatic stencil printer with 2D vision alignment that deposits solder paste onto PCB pads with repeatable accuracy down to 25-micron offset. Handles fine-pitch component pads on computing boards where paste volume consistency directly determines solder joint reliability.

High-Speed Pick-and-Place Machine — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A multi-head turret placement system capable of placing tens of thousands of components per hour. Handles everything from 01005 passive components to large BGA processors and connectors with vision-assisted alignment, feeding from tape reels, trays, and tube feeders for full production throughput.

Convection Reflow Oven — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A full-length convection tunnel reflow oven with nitrogen inerting capability and independently controlled heating zones. Programmable thermal profiles handle lead-free SAC305 and other alloys with precise temperature uniformity across the entire board, critical for large computing panels with mixed thermal mass.

Wave Soldering Machine — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

For soldering through-hole components on mixed-technology computing boards. Features programmable wave parameters, automated flux application, and preheating stages. Handles connectors, power components, and mechanical through-hole parts that cannot be reflow soldered alongside surface mount components.

Selective Soldering System — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A programmable soldering system with a miniature solder wave or iron tip that targets individual through-hole joints on densely populated boards. Used where wave soldering would damage nearby SMT components, which is common on modern computing boards with mixed-technology layouts.

3D Automated Optical Inspection — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A post-reflow 3D AOI system that measures solder joint height and geometry, detects bridges, insufficient solder, tombstoned components, polarity errors, and placement offset. Inspects every board at line speed to maintain production quality standards for computing hardware.

X-Ray Inspection System — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A 2D/3D X-ray imaging system for inspecting hidden solder joints under BGA processors, QFN packages, and other bottom-terminated components. Essential for computing boards where the most critical connections on CPUs, GPUs, and chipsets are completely hidden from optical inspection.

In-Circuit & Flying Probe Test — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Electrical test platforms that verify component presence, correct values, solder joint integrity, and basic circuit function on every assembled board. In-circuit test uses a bed-of-nails fixture for high-volume runs while flying probe handles lower volumes and prototypes without custom fixturing.

BGA Rework Station — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

An IR and hot-air rework system with bottom-side preheat and top-side vision alignment for removing and replacing BGA and CSP packages on assembled boards. Enables recovery of expensive computing boards with single-component failures without scrapping the entire assembly.

Conformal Coating Line — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

An automated selective or spray conformal coating system that applies protective acrylic, silicone, or urethane polymer coatings over assembled computing boards. Provides moisture, dust, and chemical resistance for boards deployed in industrial, automotive, or harsh operating environments.

Thermal & Environmental Test Chamber — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A programmable temperature and humidity cycling chamber for accelerated life testing and reliability qualification of finished computing boards. Simulates years of thermal stress in days to verify solder joint integrity, component survival, and long-term performance before product release.

DI Water Purification System — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Produces ultra-pure deionized water required by the plating, etching, cleaning, and rinsing processes throughout the fabrication line. Prevents ionic contamination that would compromise copper adhesion, solder mask adhesion, and surface finish quality on production computing boards.

Chemical Waste Treatment System — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

Processes spent etchant solutions, plating bath chemistry, and contaminated rinse water to meet environmental discharge regulations. Recovers copper and neutralizes acids for safe disposal, which is mandatory for any production PCB facility handling the volume of chemistry a million-dollar lab generates.

Industrial Fume Extraction & Air Handling — $500 / mo Subscription Plan

A centralized extraction and filtration system that captures chemical vapors from etching and plating, solder fumes from reflow and wave, and particulates from drilling and routing. Maintains OSHA-compliant air quality across the entire production floor for safe continuous operation.

For a single $500 monthly subscription, your team receives access to our complete open-source machine ecosystem: mechanical assemblies, electronics modules, firmware references, control architectures, and continuously updated part libraries.
